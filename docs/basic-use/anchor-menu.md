# The Anchor Menu

While in [App], tapping on the Anchor opens a menu with three options. This menu is **visible only to you** — other users in the room are not affected.

---

## Leave Room

Use this to switch rooms without restarting the app.

- Grab the red **"Leave Room"** cube
- Enter or scan a new room code when prompted

This is useful if you accidentally joined the wrong room.

---

## Close Menu

Closes the Anchor menu without taking any action. Tapping the Anchor again also closes the menu.

---

## Reposition Center for Me

Use this if your Anchor is misaligned from the physical floor marker in your room. This adjusts the Anchor **for your device only** — other users are not affected.

1. Grab the blue **"Reposition Center for Me"** cube — this unlocks the floor Anchor for your device
2. Follow the [Manual Placement](../getting-started/room-setup.md#manual-placement) steps to move and re-lock the Anchor
